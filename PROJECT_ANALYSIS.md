# HookMind: The Cognitive Layer for Uniswap v4

**Executive Summary**: HookMind is an institutional-grade protocol that solves the "Static Asset Management" problem in DeFi. By utilizing Uniswap v4 Hooks and an off-chain AI Swarm, we provide dynamic fee optimization and Impermanent Loss (IL) insurance, turning passive liquidity into an active, self-optimizing asset.

---

## 1. The Problem: The "Silent Tax" of Static DEXs
Traditional AMMs (Uniswap V2/V3) suffer from two critical flaws:
- **Reactive Loss**: Fees don't adjust to volatility. Arbitrageurs extract value from LPs during price spikes.
- **Inertia**: Governance cannot react at the speed of the market (block-level).

## 2. The Solution: Cognitive Liquidity
HookMind separates the **Cognitive Layer** (AI) from the **Execution Layer** (V4 Hook):
- **AI-Driven Fees**: Off-chain agents monitor Unichain mempools and adjust fees every 12 seconds.
- **IL Insurance**: A risk-pooled insurance layer that compensates LPs for impermanent loss, funded by performance tolls and premiums.
- **Audit Mesh**: Every AI decision is cryptographically signed and stored on IPFS via the official **Uniswap AI** framework.

## 3. Market Opportunity & Traction
- **Target Market**: Institutional LPs, treasury managers, and retail yield seekers on Unichain.
- **Competitive Edge**: First mover in AI-native v4 hooks. Integration with the official Uniswap Trading API for superior routing.
- **Traction**: Participant in the **Uniswap Foundation Acceleration Season**. Prototype deployed on Unichain Sepolia.

## 4. Revenue Model (B2B2C)
1. **Infrastructure Access**: 0.00244 ETH node activation fee.
2. **Performance Fee**: 5-10% of "Alpha" generated by dynamic fee adjustments.
3. **Insurance Premiums**: Recurring USDC revenue from protected pools.
